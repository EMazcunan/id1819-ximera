\maketitle

Hint environments can be embedded inside problems.

\begin{problem}
  What is $2+2$ equal to?
  \begin{hint}
    Name a number greater than $3$ but less than $5$. The number I'm
    thinking of is $\answer{4}$.
  \end{hint}
  \[
  2+2 = \answer{4}
  \]
\end{problem}
